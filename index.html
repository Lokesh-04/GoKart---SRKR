The SRKR Go-Kart Championship is an exhilarating competition aimed at fostering creativity and technical skills among emerging engineers. This event, carried out in SRKR Engineering College, assures teams complete design leeway and helps them indulge in their imagination as much as possible towards the creation of high speed racing go-karts. With a few critical guidelines in place, the contest active encourages the competitors to explore the limits of the design, engineering, and performance pyramids. In the SRKR Go-Kart Championship, each team shall act as an engine development company which is commissioned with the task of designing, constructing and commissioning a prototype go-kart for the marketplace of non-professional go-kart racing. The teams shall aim to design, build, test and showcase karts with outstanding performance in speed, braking, handling and structural performance. The purpose of this Championship is to design and build a go-kart that will be competitive in all the extremes postulated in the SRKR Championship Rulebook and a reasonably priced product for the weekend racers who want excitement and dependability. This challenge not only assesses participant's technical abilities. It is also an experience which encourages them to think critically, be inventive and work effectively with others in order to deliver their ideas on the track. Come along for this exciting spectacle where desire for engineering is married with love for racing.
